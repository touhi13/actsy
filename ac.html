ACTSY – A REPUTABLE SERVICE PROVIDER WITH DEEP INDUSTRY EXPERTISE

We are a full-service CPA and consulting firm that brings nearly 30 years of accounting experience and know-how to help make sense of the numbers. Our accounting services assist companies with amassing all necessary accounting data to determine profitability, executing successful strategies, and focusing on growth objectives. At Actsy, we are committed to providing the most accurate and timely information to our clients, while being proactive in identifying issues and opportunities. Our goal is to help businesses achieve their goals by providing them with actionable advice, customized solutions, and a deep understanding of their business needs.

  2. Financial Planning & Analysis
  3. Accounts Payable bill entry and payment issuance
  4. Accounts Receivable invoicing and payment posting
  5. Payroll administration including setup, processing, tax filings and payments
  6. Sales tax preparation and filing
  7. Transaction data importing, classification, and reconciliation
  8. Financial statement preparation
